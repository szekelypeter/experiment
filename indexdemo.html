Our Story

What is CARP 3D? The CARP 3D is a cheap, and rapid 3D printer, which operates out of the box, is mainenance-free, and availeable for everyone.

How did we do this? It's simple: we aimed for balance. Most 3D printers are either an overkill, with thick timing belts, large motors, heavy extruder assembly, bulky adn expensive linear technology, and overly complex solutions, OR go for the cheapest in everything, so they use the cheapest steppers, cheap electronics, no linear guides, etc.

We found a way to incorporate the best of everything. We don't use expensive linear rails, nor wide timing belts, instead we reduced the load on them. CARP uses big, and strong steppers where it's necessary, and cost effective ones where they don't compromise the performance. We have custom electronics, so it's more stable than an arduino based solution -like ramps-, while it's also cheaper. Our mechanical structure is also simple. We are using as few parts as possible, most of them designed specifically for CARP (there are less than 25 different parts including screws, washers, and nuts). This means that CARP 3D is easy to manufacture, helping us to reduce labour costs.

Specifications

Build volume: 3L+

Maximal printing speed: 150mm/s

Software: custom (also works with standard g-code)

Weigth: ~6.5lbs/3kg (so expect low shipping costs)

Filament type: standard 1.75mm filament, 190mm spool diameter max

Power consumption: <60W

Retail price:(TBA $300 maximum )

Printable materials: Tested with PLA. ABS, HIPS, coming soon.
